Discover Natural Healing

Water is essential for life, but not all water is the same. In the world of wellness and health, two popular choices stand out: alkaline water and spring water. While both are often marketed as healthy options, they differ in origin, composition, and potential health benefits.

Alkaline water is water that has a higher pH level than regular drinking water. While normal water has a neutral pH of around 7, alkaline water typically has a pH of 8 or 9. This increased alkalinity is achieved either naturally (from mineral springs) or artificially (through ionization or by adding minerals like calcium, magnesium, and potassium)
